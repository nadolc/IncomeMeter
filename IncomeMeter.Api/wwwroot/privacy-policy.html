Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

IncomeMeter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our income tracking application and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number (optional)
  • Profile Information: User preferences, settings, currency, language
  • Route Data: Work schedules, route information, mileage data
  • Income Data: Income amounts, sources, transaction details
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for route tracking (when permitted)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, error logs

2.3 Third-Party Information

  • Google OAuth: Basic profile information when you sign in with Google
  • Gmail Integration: Email data for income detection (with explicit permission)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our income tracking services
  • Calculate income statistics and generate reports
  • Track routes and mileage for work-related activities
  • Personalize your experience and preferences
  • Send important service notifications
  • Improve our application and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate (MongoDB, Azure, etc.)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfer: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

5. Data Security

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication with JWT tokens
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and audit logging
  • Azure cloud security infrastructure

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Route and Income Data: Retained for tax and record-keeping purposes (up to 7 years)
  • Log Data: Retained for 90 days for troubleshooting
  • After Account Deletion: Most data deleted within 30 days

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Opt-out: Disable location tracking or notifications

8. Cookies and Tracking

  • Authentication Cookies: To keep you logged in
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings
  • Analytics: To understand app usage (anonymized)

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Azure global security standards
  • Data Processing Agreements with service providers

10. Children's Privacy

IncomeMeter is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the application. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
